// About Us

Building the future of intelligent enterprise

SAIES is a student-led organization dedicated to exploring how AI technologies are reshaping modern enterprise systems — from supply chain optimization to intelligent CRM, from automated financial modeling to AI-driven product strategy.

We host workshops, speaker series, case competitions, and hands-on project labs that give members direct experience applying AI to real business problems. Whether you're a CS major building models or a business major asking the hard questions about deployment — there's a place for you here.

We partner with Fortune 500 companies, fast-growing startups, and leading research labs to ensure our members are working on problems that actually matter.

01
Learn
Deep dives into enterprise AI through workshops, reading groups, and technical bootcamps led by members and industry professionals.
02
Build
Semester-long project teams tackling real enterprise challenges — from deploying LLMs to automating complex workflows.
03
Connect
A direct pipeline to internships and full-time roles at companies leading enterprise AI adoption and transformation.
04
Debate
Critical conversations about AI governance, ethics, and the societal implications of deploying AI in large organizations.

What we actually work on

AI Strategy & Transformation
